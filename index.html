some guiding rules (just few):
Nº 01
KINDNESS

Be kind, even in the most cynical situations.

Especially to the Reviewer 2 living in your head.
Nº 02
BOUNDARIES

Learn to say no, within the bounds of your kindness.

"No, thank you" is two whole words. Very usable.
Nº 03
LEAN IN

Ask for help. It does not have to be only your fight.

Advisors exist. Friends exist. That person three years ahead of you also exists.
Nº 04
NOURISH

Three meals. Breakfast, lunch, early dinner. One meat-free.

You cannot out-brain hunger. Just go eat.
Nº 05
PRESENCE

Listen with empathy. Don't talk about yourself unless it helps.

Nobody came for your anecdote. Yet.
Nº 06
HUMILITY

The world is full of smarter people. Look up — while admiring how far you've come.

Awe and self-respect are not opposites.
Nº 07
PEOPLE

Connect with loved ones. Reach out to the friends you've lost touch with.

Dad texted. Tuesday. Just saying.
Nº 08
MOVE

Run if you can. Walk 35 minutes every other day. Persist.

The chair has held you long enough today.
Nº 09
FINISH

Finish the books you already own before buying new ones.

The TBR pile is disappointed.
Nº 10
MAKE

Finish the parallel papers. Learn a language. Take up drawing.

A hobby is how you remember you are not just a PhD.
Today's focus · chosen by the universe!
Choose one rule and let it shape the day.
— rotating daily
Built slowly, kept honestly. May the remaining days be full of the good kind of tired.
